Our Mission

Production reliability through data, not guesswork. ความน่าเชื่อถือทางการผลิตด้วยข้อมูล ไม่ใช่การคาดเดา

BFLFP runs a complex production line where unplanned downtime, missed PMs, and slow response times directly impact output. The Maintenance Control Center exists to give every level of the team — operators, planners, technicians, supervisors — the right view of the right data, at the right time.

From a single source of truth in SharePoint to live KPIs in the browser, this system replaces spreadsheets, paper logs, and tribal knowledge with structured, auditable maintenance data.

What we measure

  • MTTR — Mean Time To Repair
  • MTBF — Mean Time Between Failures
  • PM Compliance — Preventive jobs on time
  • FTFR — First Time Fix Rate
  • Breakdown Rate — Unplanned events
  • Overdue PM — Tasks past due
  • Response Time — Report → start

FAQ

Common questions คำถามที่พบบ่อย

How often does the data refresh?

Live — every time you open the Dashboard, Reports, or Maintenance Log, or click the Refresh button. There's no nightly batch, no cache.

Can I export the data?

Yes. The Dashboard, Reports, and Maintenance Log pages each have an "Export CSV" button that downloads exactly what you see, with all active filters applied.

What if a KPI shows "—"?

That means there's no data to compute it in the selected scope — for example, no completed Preventive jobs to calculate PM Compliance against. Adjust your filters or wait for jobs to be closed.

How do I add a new machine or change a category?

Add it as a record in the SharePoint list. The site automatically picks up new machines, technicians, statuses, job types, and priorities — no code changes needed.

Is the data secure?

SharePoint enforces access control on the underlying list. The web pages call a Power Automate flow that returns the items — anyone who knows the flow URL can read it, so the URL must be rotated if leaked.

Who maintains this system?

The BFLFP IT / Maintenance team. For data issues, contact the maintenance lead. For technical issues, contact IT.
